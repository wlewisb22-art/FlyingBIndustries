FAQ

Common questions before requesting a quote.

Can you print or engrave on items I provide?

Usually yes, as long as the item is compatible, clean, and fits the equipment. Some customer-provided items may require testing or may carry a risk because custom work cannot always be undone.

What artwork works best?

High-resolution PNG, JPG, SVG, PDF, or AI files are best. Transparent PNGs are great for logos. If all you have is a photo or rough idea, send it and we’ll review what can be done.

Are the decals waterproof?

The UV decals are intended for waterproof and outdoor-style use, but actual long-term life depends on the surface, prep, exposure, abrasion, and installation. They are a strong option when direct printing is not practical.

Can you help choose between UV printing, engraving, and decals?

Yes. That is the point of the quote process. Send the item and the use case, and we’ll recommend the method that makes the most sense.

Have an item in mind?

Send the idea, item, material, size, quantity, and deadline. We’ll tell you what production method makes the most sense and send a quote.
