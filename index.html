— 01 / About

From one golfer, to another.

Hi — I'm the guy behind Flying B Industries. I love golf, and there's something genuinely fun about playing with a ball that's yours. Your dog's face. An inside joke with your buddies. A logo for the next tournament. A photo of someone you miss. It always beats a plain factory ball.

I started this because I thought it was a blast to have my own custom balls to play with and show off — and I figured plenty of other golfers would feel the same way. Now I make them for funny logos, special events, gifts, memorials, club tournaments, and pretty much anything else you can think of. Whatever your reason, I'm happy to make it happen.

Every order goes through me personally. I'll answer your questions, send a proof before anything gets printed, and make sure it turns out the way you pictured it. No call center, no portal, no upsells. Just one golfer making custom balls for other golfers — and for anyone who wants to put a smile on a golfer's face.

And honestly? Knowing your funny logo might end up in someone else's pocket someday after a wayward drive into the rough — that's part of the fun too. — Flying B Industries

— 05 / Email Checklist

What to include in your email.

The more detail you give us up front, the faster we can get you an accurate quote.

  • Total Quantity How many balls in total do you need produced?
  • Ball Preference Option A, B, or C above. If Option B, tell us the exact brand and model.
  • Your Image(s) Attach as PNG or JPG, highest resolution available. Transparent background preferred for logos.
  • Layout (if multiple images) See note below on how to specify multi-image orders.
  • Deadline When do you need them by? Helpful for events, tournaments, or gifts.
  • Shipping Address Where should we send the finished balls?
  • Your Name & Phone So we can follow up quickly with questions.
  • Occasion (Optional) Wedding, corporate event, tournament, gift — context helps.

Sending more than one image?

Please tell us which layout you want:

Option 1: Two images on opposite sides of the same ball.

Option 2: Different images on different balls — in this case, tell us how many balls you want for each image (e.g., 12 with Image A, 12 with Image B).

A quick note on artwork rights

Please only send images you own or have permission to use. We can't reproduce copyrighted characters, sports team logos, or trademarked artwork without proof of authorization.

How pricing works

Quotes are based on quantity, ball choice (your own / specific brand / Kirkland), artwork complexity, and shipping. There's no minimum order — we'll price small orders accordingly. You'll always see the full price before you commit, and we'll never add hidden fees later.

Sales tax is collected on orders shipped within Georgia, as required by state law. Out-of-state orders are not currently subject to sales tax.

— 07 / Common Questions

Things people ask us.

Don't see your question here? Email us — we're happy to help.

How long does it take to get my golf balls?

Turnaround depends on the size of your order, the ball option you choose, and our current production schedule. We'll give you a realistic timeline along with your quote. If you have a hard deadline (event, gift date, tournament), let us know in your email so we can confirm we can hit it.

Will I see a proof before you print?

Yes — for most orders we'll send you a digital mockup of how your image will look on the ball before we produce anything. You'll have a chance to approve or request adjustments before we move forward.

How is pricing determined?

Quotes are based on quantity, ball choice (your own balls, a specific brand we source, or budget-friendly Kirkland), artwork complexity, and shipping. There's no minimum order — we'll price small orders accordingly. You'll always see the full price before committing, and there are no hidden fees added later.

Can I return or refund a custom order?

Because every order is custom-made, approved orders are generally not returnable — the proof-approval step is what protects you, since once you've signed off on the design, we begin printing on materials that can't be repurposed. That said, if there's a production error, a quality issue, or the finished balls don't match the proof you approved, contact us and we'll make it right — repair, replacement, or refund as appropriate.

Is there a minimum order?

Reach out with the quantity you have in mind and we'll let you know what works. Smaller orders are welcome — we'll just price them accordingly.

What kind of image works best?

High-resolution PNG or JPG files produce the best results. For logos and graphics, a PNG with a transparent background is ideal. For photos, the higher the resolution the better. If you're unsure whether your image will work well, send it over and we'll take a look.

Can you print copyrighted or trademarked images?

Only with proof of authorization. We can't reproduce things like sports team logos, cartoon characters, or other trademarked artwork without permission from the rights holder. Your own logos, photos, and original artwork are no problem.

How do I pay?

Payment details will be included with your quote. We'll walk you through the process once you decide to move forward — no payment needed just to get a quote.

Can I reorder later?

Absolutely. We keep records of your order so reorders are simple — just email us and reference your previous order.

— Policies & Terms

Proofs, returns, and peace of mind.

Digital Proof Before Production

Before we print anything, we send you a digital mockup showing exactly how your image will appear on the ball. Nothing goes into production until you approve the layout. If something doesn't look right, just say so — we'll adjust until it does.

Returns on Custom Orders

Because every order is custom-made, approved orders are generally not returnable. The proof-approval step is what protects you here — once you've signed off on the design, we begin printing on materials that can't be repurposed for anyone else.

That said, we stand behind our work. If there's a production error, a quality issue, or the finished balls don't match the proof you approved, contact us and we'll make it right — repair, replacement, or refund as appropriate to the situation.

Order Changes & Cancellations

Need to change quantity, swap an image, or cancel? Reach out as soon as possible. Changes and cancellations are usually possible before you approve the proof — once production begins, options are more limited.

Shipping & Delivery

We ship throughout the United States. Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number by email. Standard shipping is via USPS or UPS, with the carrier chosen based on package size and your location.

Delivery times vary by carrier and destination, typically 3–7 business days within the continental US after shipping. Expedited options are available — let us know if you have a hard deadline and we'll work something out.

If a package is lost or damaged in transit, we'll work with you and the carrier to resolve it (file claims, replace the order if confirmed lost, etc.). International orders are by request only — please contact us before placing one.

Color & Proof Accuracy

Colors may appear slightly different on the printed ball than they do on your screen. This is due to monitor calibration, the printing process, and the way ink interacts with the ball's surface. The digital proof we send is the closest representation of how the final product will look.

If exact color matching is critical for your project (for example, matching a specific corporate brand color), let us know in your initial email so we can discuss whether we can accommodate it.

Image Quality & Resolution

The print quality of your finished balls depends entirely on the quality of the image you provide. Low-resolution, blurry, or pixelated source files will produce low-resolution, blurry, or pixelated prints — there's no way to add detail that wasn't there to begin with.

For best results: send the highest-resolution PNG or JPG you have. For logos and graphics, vector files (SVG, AI, EPS) or PNGs with transparent backgrounds are ideal. For photos, original camera or phone files are better than screenshots or images saved from social media.

If we have concerns about whether your file will produce a good result, we'll let you know before sending the proof so you have a chance to provide a better version.

Damaged Shipments

If your shipment arrives visibly damaged, please contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos of the packaging and the damaged contents. This helps us file a claim with the carrier and arrange a replacement quickly.

Reports made after 7 days are harder to resolve because most carriers require timely claims, but we'll always do our best to help.

Limitation of Liability

In the event of a quality issue, production error, or other problem with your order, our maximum liability is the total amount you paid for the affected portion of the order.

Flying B Industries is not responsible for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages — for example, costs related to events, gifts, or other purposes the balls were intended for. We do everything we can to deliver on time and as expected, but ultimately the balls themselves are what we're responsible for.

Right to Decline Orders

We reserve the right to decline any order for any reason, including but not limited to: requests involving copyrighted or trademarked content without proper authorization, content we consider offensive or inappropriate, files we cannot produce to our quality standards, or requests we're unable to fulfill on the requested timeline.

If we decline an order, we'll let you know promptly and refund any payment that's already been made.

Production Delays & Force Majeure

Most orders ship on schedule, but production timelines can occasionally be affected by factors outside our control: ball availability from suppliers (especially for Option B specific-brand orders), shipping carrier delays, weather events, equipment issues, or other unforeseen circumstances.

If a delay affects your order, we'll communicate as soon as we're aware. For time-sensitive orders, please share your deadline up front so we can confirm whether we can meet it before you commit.

— Your Privacy

How we handle your information.

What we collect. When you email us a request, we receive whatever you choose to send — typically your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, the images you want printed, and details about your order.

What we use it for. We use your information for one purpose only: producing and delivering your custom golf balls and communicating with you about your order. That's it.

What we don't do. We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your information with any third parties. We do not add you to marketing lists. We do not send promotional emails or advertisements. We do not use your contact information to follow up with offers, and we do not share your images or order details with anyone outside of fulfilling your specific order.

Your images. The artwork and photos you send remain yours. We use them only to produce your order. We do not display, share, or reuse your images for marketing or any other purpose without your explicit permission.

Records. We keep order records so we can support reorders, address any quality concerns, and meet basic business and tax requirements. If you ever want your information removed from our records, just email us and we'll take care of it.

Service providers. The only third parties involved in your order are the ones required to fulfill it — for example, our shipping carrier, or, if you choose Option B, the brand we source your balls from. They receive only the information needed to do their part (like a shipping address) and nothing more.

Questions. If you have any questions about how we handle your information, just ask: orders@flyingbindustries.com
