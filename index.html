— 02 / About

One person, one shop.

Flying B Industries started with a single golf ball. A buddy wanted his dog's face printed on a sleeve for his birthday, and I couldn't find anyone willing to do small runs without charging the price of a new driver.

So I bought the equipment, learned the craft, and started doing it myself. Then friends asked about tumblers. Then hats. Then waterproof decals for their boats. Now we do all of it — same hands, same attention to detail, same "yes" to small orders.

Every piece that goes out has been touched, checked, and approved by me. That's the whole pitch. — The Flying B
— 03 / The Process

Three steps from idea to delivery.

Same process whether it's one golf ball or fifty leather patches.

01

Send your idea

Email us with what you're thinking — what you want personalized, how many, and any artwork or reference images. No special file format required; we'll work with what you have.

02

We send a proof

We turn your idea into a digital mockup so you can see exactly how it'll look on the finished product. You approve, we tweak, repeat until it's right.

03

We make and ship

Once you approve the proof and pricing, we produce the order by hand and ship it to your door. Most projects ship within 5–10 business days of approval.
