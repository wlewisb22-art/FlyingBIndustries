— 01 / Two Methods

Laser engraving or UV print?

Both look great. They look different. Here's how to think about it.

Method A · Laser Engraving

Etched, permanent, monochrome

The laser literally burns the design into the tumbler's coating, revealing the steel underneath. Result: a clean, two-tone look that won't peel, chip, fade, or wash off — ever. Best for logos, monograms, names, simple line art. No color, just contrast.

Method B · UV Printing

Full color, photos, gradients

A UV printer lays full-color ink directly onto the tumbler and cures it instantly under UV light. Result: vivid, sharp, photo-quality designs in any color combination. Best for logos with multiple colors, photographs, gradients, and detailed artwork. Durable but not quite as bombproof as laser etching.

Not sure which to pick? Tell us about your design and we'll recommend one.

— 05 / Common Questions

Things people ask us.

Are engraved/printed tumblers dishwasher safe?

Laser-engraved tumblers are fully dishwasher safe — the engraving is etched into the surface and can't wash off. UV-printed tumblers are top-rack dishwasher safe but for maximum longevity we recommend hand-washing. Either way, the design will hold up to normal daily use.

Can you engrave on the bottom of my tumbler?

Yes — bottom engraving is a nice touch for gifts (think "Established 2024" or initials). Just let us know if you want both the side and the bottom done.

Will it scratch off?

Laser engraving doesn't scratch off because it's not a coating — the laser actually removes material. UV ink is very durable and resistant to scratching, but a sharp object dragged across it with force could damage it (same as any printed surface).

Can you do photos on a tumbler?

Yes — full-color photos via UV printing. The result is sharp and vivid. Higher-resolution source photos produce better prints; we'll let you know if a photo isn't high enough quality before we print.

What sizes do you work with?

Most common sizes: 12oz, 20oz, 30oz tumblers and 24–40oz water bottles. We can also do larger jugs and growlers — send us the brand/model and we'll confirm.

How long does it take?

Single-tumbler orders typically turn around in 3–7 business days. Larger orders (50+) usually 1–2 weeks. We'll confirm timing when we send your quote.

What about copyrighted logos and characters?

Only with proof of authorization. We can't reproduce things like sports team logos, Disney characters, or other trademarked artwork without permission from the rights holder.

Can I do designs that wrap all the way around the tumbler?

Yes, full-wrap UV-printed designs are possible. There may be a small seam where the print starts and ends. Laser engraving works best in defined zones rather than continuous wraps.
