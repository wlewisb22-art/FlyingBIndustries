— 01 / Leather Types

Three kinds of leather we work with.

Each has a different feel and look. We'll recommend based on what you're going for, or you can pick.

Default

Genuine Leather

Our standard option. Smooth, durable, takes engraving cleanly with strong contrast. Best for sharp logos, text, and modern designs. Ages well with handling.

Premium

Vegetable-Tanned

A heritage tannage that develops a rich patina over time. Lighter starting tone, deeper engraving contrast, and a more traditional look. Great for ranch brands and rustic designs.

Specialty

Suede

Softer, more textured. The engraving comes out as a subtle two-tone effect rather than the high-contrast burn of smooth leather. A great alternative look when you want something less polished.

— 02 / How It Works

Three ways to order.

Pick what fits where you're at.

Option A

Hats + Patches, Attached

Pick your hat style and leather type, we'll engrave the patches, attach them to the hats, and ship the finished product. Most popular option for gifts and events.

Bring Your Own Hats

Already have hats? Ship them to us, we'll engrave patches and attach them. Useful if you have a specific hat style or color we don't typically stock.

Option C

If you want to handle attachment yourself (or have a hat-maker you work with), we'll just engrave and ship the patches. They come ready to be sewn or applied with leather adhesive.

— 05 / Common Questions

Things people ask us.

What hat styles work best with leather patches?

Richardson 112 trucker hats are the most popular by a wide margin — they have the right front panel for a clean patch placement. Snapbacks, dad hats, and flat brim caps also work well. If you have a specific hat in mind, send us the make and model and we'll confirm.

Can you make patches in custom shapes?

Yes — most patches we cut are rectangular or rounded-rectangle "shield" shapes, but we can do circles, ovals, custom contours, even die-cut shapes that follow the outline of your logo. Just let us know the look you're going for.

How are the patches attached to the hats?

For most orders we use a permanent leather adhesive that bonds the patch to the hat front. For larger orders or specialty requests, we can also stitch patches. The adhesive method is fast, durable, and won't damage the hat fabric.

Will the engraving fade over time?

The engraving is permanent — it's burned into the leather. The leather itself will darken and develop a natural patina with handling and use, which most people find improves the look. The design won't fade or wash off.

Can I get just a few patches for samples?

Yes. There's no minimum. We're happy to do single-patch or small-batch orders if you want to see the result before ordering a larger run.

How long does production take?

Single-patch and small orders typically turn around in 5–7 business days. Larger orders (25+ hats) usually 1–2 weeks. We'll confirm timing when we send your quote.

Can you reproduce my exact logo?

If you own the artwork (or have authorization), yes. We can engrave from PNG, JPG, SVG, AI, or PDF files. For logos with very fine detail, vector files work best so the engraving stays sharp. We can't reproduce trademarked logos (sports teams, characters, brands) without permission from the rights holder.

What if I don't have a digital file of my design?

Send us a photo, a sketch, or even a description and we'll help you get to a usable design. We can clean up existing artwork or work from rough references.
