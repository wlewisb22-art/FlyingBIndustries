What to send

Help us quote it accurately.

The more details you include up front, the faster we can tell you if this method is the right fit.

Item details

Tell us the item, material, dimensions, print/engrave area, quantity, and whether you are providing the item or need us to source it.

Artwork

Attach your logo, photo, PNG, JPG, SVG, PDF, screenshot, sketch, or reference image. High-resolution files work best.

Use case

Tell us whether it will be handled, washed, displayed outdoors, put on equipment, given as a gift, or used for business promotion.

Important note

Every material behaves differently.

Surface compatibility matters. Some materials need testing, cleaning, adhesion promoter, or a different approach such as a waterproof decal.
